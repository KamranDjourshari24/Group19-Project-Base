About This Website

As home entertainment continues to rise in popularity and more films than ever before are being produced, the need to find the right movie becomes more difficult for most movie fanatics. The search for an intriguing movie to watch leaves many users frustrated browsing for something that fits their interests. Some seem to face information overload with too many movies to choose from, which only fatigues people from watching anything.

A way to browse movies condensed with only the most necessary information stated for every movie on a simple and easy-to-follow website. Some can also be influenced by what other movie fanatics might have reviewed/ commented on the film, which we hope to include within our application as well. We allow users to use advanced filtering for different categories (i.e. actors, nominations, rating, genre) so viewers can choose a movie from a limited list that fits their criteria. These potential features can provide us a solution to the information problem as our application will primarily focus on providing the necessary content for the audience to make a decision and not feel overwhelmed when making a decision.

Our database allows users to add movies to our database. If you movie exists you may see it up on one of pages. Try it out by clicking the button below!
